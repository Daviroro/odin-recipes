Chilli Sauce

I've looked this recipe up so many times i've lost count. Experiment with different Chillies and amend other ingredients accordingly

Makes 2 Litres

Ingredients

  • 4 onions
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 20 fresh mild red chillies
  • olive oil
  • 4 teaspoons chilli powder
  • 1 kg ripe tomatoes
  • 300 ml cider vinegar
  • 80 g golden caster sugar
  • 500 ml unsweetened apple juice
  1. chop the onions, garlic and chillies and soften in a pan with some oil
  2. add the chilli powder, 200ml of water, vinegar, sugar and apple juice. Bring to boil and simmer for 20 mins
  3. Blend it up with a stick blender, possibly a little extra garlic and seasoning, then simmer until it looks like tomato soup
