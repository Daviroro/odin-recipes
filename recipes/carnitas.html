Carnitas

Some slow cooker flavoursome meat

Use any meat you fancy but try to keep to quite fatty cuts and similar amounts

Ingredients

  • 2kg meat
  • Some oil (for the rub)
  • Tbsp Oregano
  • Tbsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Chili
  • Tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • An Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • Juice from one Orange
  1. Make a rub from all the seasoning and garlic
  2. Rub the meat with the seasoning
  3. Chop up onion
  4. Put the meat in the slow cooker, surround it with onions
  5. Pour Orange juice over meat
  6. Cook for 8-10 hours on low or 5-6 on high
  7. Take the meat out, save the juices for later. Shred the meat with some forks and spread it on a grill pan
  8. Pour liquid over the meat and grill it, adding more liquid as it dries out
