Vietnamese Lemongrass Pork

Really nice Vietnamese recipe, other meats will work. Serve with an Asian salad

Ingredients

  • 1.8kg Pork
  • coriander and lime to serve
For the marinade
  • 6 large cloves garlic
  • 2 large shallots
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 3/4 cup Fish Sauce
  • 3 tbsp pineapple juice, Orange Juice also works
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  1. Cut the pork into strips or cubes
  2. Blend all the marinade ingredients together
  3. Coat the pork with the marinade and leave for at least 4 hours
  4. Thread the meat onto skewers
  5. Grill the skewers until almost burnt
