Pizza Dough

I'm constantly looking up the ratio of flour to water for this - my cookbook opens automatically to this page

Makes 4 Pizzas

Ingredients

  • Packet of 7g dried yeast (10g if you can find any fresh stuff)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 320ml of warm water
  • 500g pizza flour (mix of 00 and strong white)
  • 7g of salt
  • Oil and extrra flour for kneading
  • Semolina/polenta/cornmeal for baking
  1. Mix the yeast, water and sugar together and let sit until a bit bubbly
  2. Weigh the flour and salt into a big bowl. Mix
  3. Combine the liquid with dough just until a dough comes together
  4. Tip out to a floured or oiled surface and knead until the dough comes alive (usually about 10mins)
  5. Make sure the mixing bowl is clean and coat with Olive oil
  6. Put the dough in the oiled bowl, cover and leave in a warm place to rise

  7. After about 2 hours take out the dough and knock back a bit

    Divide into 4 pieces and wrap really loosely in cling film, keep in fridge until you need to use it (will last at most 2 days, you can try more but that's all i'll risk)

  8. To use dust flat surface with semolina. Roll out and do some spinning (mainly for fun). try to get it as thin as possible

  9. If you've got a pizza oven awesome, you probably know how it works

    Otherwise turn your oven up as high it goes (mine can get to about 350 degrees C), cook your pizza (toppings your choice) on a preheated tray

    for about 10 mins or until the dough is crispy an slightly brown in places (might be longer or shorter depending on your oven)
